INTRODUCTION:

Sichuan University, The university was formed by the merger of three national key universities: Sichuan University, Chengdu University of Science and Technology and West China University of Medical Sciences. The former Sichuan University was founded in 1896 by Lu Chuanlin, Governor of Sichuan Province, at the special order of Guangxu. It was the earliest modern institution of higher learning in Southwest China. The former Chengdu University of Science and Technology was one of the first batch of multi-subject engineering colleges established during the restructuring of the new China. The original West China University of Medical Science originated from West China United University in Chengdu, which was founded by a Western Christian Church in 1910. It was one of the earliest Western-style universities in Southwest China and one of the earliest universities to train graduate students in China. 1994 The former Sichuan University and the former Chengdu University of Science and Technology were merged into Sichuan United University, and the name Sichuan University was restored in 1998. 2000 It merged with the former West China University of Medical Sciences to form the new Sichuan University.
